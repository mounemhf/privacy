## 2. What information we collect

**The Game does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or any third party.**

The Game operates entirely offline. There are no accounts, no online leaderboards, no in-game purchases, no advertising, and no analytics SDKs.

### Locally stored data (on your device only)

The Game saves the following information to your local device using the standard Unity `PlayerPrefs` system. This data **never leaves your device** and is **not accessible to us**:

| Stored value | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Total coins collected | Track in-game progression |
| Current selected level | Resume from the last selected level |
| Highest level unlocked | Unlock progression |
| Number of stars earned per level | Score tracking |
| Sound on/off preference | Audio preference |
| Difficulty mode (Easy / Medium) | Gameplay setting |

This data is stored in a sandboxed location managed by the Microsoft Store / Windows / Xbox runtime. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the Game or by clearing the app's local data via Windows Settings.

## 6. Third-party components

The Game uses the following third-party libraries / engines, all of which run **locally** on your device and do not transmit data to their authors or to us:

- **Unity Engine** (game engine)
- **Rewired** (input handling library)
- **X-UniTMX** (Tiled map importer)
- **MoreMountains Corgi Engine** (2D platformer toolkit)

None of these components include online tracking or telemetry as configured in this Game.

## 7. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for you to access, rectify, delete, or port under GDPR, CCPA, or similar regulations.

If you wish to remove the locally stored game progression data:

1. Open **Windows Settings → Apps → Super Jumpman Adventures → Advanced options**
2. Click **Reset** (this clears local data)
3. Or uninstall the Game

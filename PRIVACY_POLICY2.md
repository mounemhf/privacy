# Privacy Policy — Super Ninja Adventure

**Effective date:** May 11, 2026

---

## 1. Scope

This privacy policy applies to the **Super Ninja Adventure** game (the "App"), published by **Fun Studio Games**, available on the Microsoft Store and Xbox Store.

## 2. Data we do not collect

Super Ninja Adventure **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** about you. Specifically:

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
- We do **not** track your location.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs (Google AdMob, Unity Ads, IronSource, AppLovin, etc.).
- We do **not** use third-party analytics SDKs (Unity Analytics, Firebase, GameAnalytics, etc.).
- We do **not** transmit any data over the network.
- We do **not** use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

## 3. Data stored locally

Super Ninja Adventure stores game progress locally on your device using Unity's `PlayerPrefs` system. This includes:

- Coins balance, ammunition (bullets, power-bullets), remaining lives
- Highest level reached, level-by-level progression
- Selected character and unlocked characters
- Sound and music preferences
- Tutorial completion flags
- Anonymous attempt counters per level

This data **never leaves your device**. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

## 4. Microsoft Store / Xbox identifier

When you download the App from the Microsoft Store or play it on Xbox, Microsoft itself may collect technical information per its own privacy policy (Microsoft account, device identifier, performance metrics). This is governed by the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement) and is independent of the App.

## 5. Children's privacy

Super Ninja Adventure does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). Since we collect no personal data at all, the App is safe for all ages.

## 6. Third-party content

Super Ninja Adventure uses the following third-party software libraries, none of which transmit personal data:

- Unity Engine (Unity Technologies)
- Rewired (Guavaman Enterprises) — input handling

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the App description on the Microsoft Store.
